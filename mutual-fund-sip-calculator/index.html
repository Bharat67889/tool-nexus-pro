What is a SIP Calculator?

A SIP Calculator helps investors estimate the future value of their mutual fund investments. SIP means Systematic Investment Plan, where you invest a fixed amount every month.

“Long-term SIP investing combined with compounding can build significant wealth over time.”

How SIP Works?

In SIP investing, you contribute a fixed amount monthly into mutual funds. Your money grows through compound returns over years.

SIP Formula

M = P × [ ( (1 + i)^n - 1 ) / i ] × (1+i)

Here:

  • P = Monthly Investment
  • i = Monthly Interest Rate
  • n = Total Number of Months

Benefits of SIP Investment

  • Disciplined investing habit
  • Power of compounding
  • Lower market timing risk
  • Affordable monthly investing
  • Long-term wealth creation
