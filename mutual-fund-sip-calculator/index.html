What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is one of the smartest ways to invest money in mutual funds regularly. Instead of investing a huge amount at once, SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount every month.

“Small investments done consistently for long periods can create massive wealth through compounding.”

SIP Formula

SIP return calculation is based on compound growth.

M = P × [ ( (1 + i)^n - 1 ) / i ] × (1+i)

Here:

  • P = Monthly Investment
  • i = Monthly Return Rate
  • n = Total Number of Months

Why SIP is Powerful?

SIP helps in rupee cost averaging and removes emotional investing mistakes. Long-term SIP investments can beat inflation and help in building wealth for goals like retirement, car, house, or financial freedom.
