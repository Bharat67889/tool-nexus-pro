BMI (Body Mass Index) Kya Hota Hai?

BMI ek aisi calculation hai jo aapke weight aur height ke hisab se batati hai ki aap fit hain ya nahi. Hamara BMI calculator aapko turant result deta hai.

BMI Categories:
18.5 se kam: Underweight | 18.5 - 24.9: Normal | 25 - 29.9: Overweight | 30+ : Obese
